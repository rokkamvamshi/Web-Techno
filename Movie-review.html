Movie Plot:

"Iron Man" aka Tony Stark, a Genius, billionaire, playboy, philantrophist and an inventor who builds a high-tech suit of armor to escape captivity.
He uses the suit to fight evil, confront his company's dark past, and transforms into the superhero Iron Man.
